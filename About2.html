About Me

My name is Matthew Posey and I am a professional massage therapist with 10 years of experience. I am excited to announce that I am now creating my own private practice to help pay my way through college and continue my education in computer science.

My area of expertise is deep tissue massage. I believe that deep tissue massage is one of the most effective ways to target specific areas of muscle tension and pain, and I am dedicated to providing my clients with the highest quality massage services to help them feel their best.

One thing that sets me apart from other massage therapists is that I have essential tremors, which cause my hands to be in a constant state of gentle vibration. I like to say that this makes me born to be a massage therapist! I believe that my condition is beneficial to how I work with the muscles. My tremor allows me to be more sensitive to the client's needs and helps me to provide a more effective massage.

I understand that everyone's needs are different, and I will work with you to create a personalized massage experience that addresses your specific needs and concerns. Whether you're looking for relaxation, stress relief, or relief from pain and muscle tension, I am here to help.

Thank you for considering my massage services. I look forward to working with you and helping you to feel your best.
